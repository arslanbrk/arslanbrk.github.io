About

I am a Doctoral Researcher in the Robotic Composites and Compositions Group at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems and the Multiscale Functional and Molecular Imaging Lab at ETH Zurich. I am a Doctoral Fellow of the Max Planck ETH Center for Learning Systems. I earned my BSc and MSc degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Bilkent University, Ankara. My current research explores magnetically actuated, untethered biomedical systems that harness jamming and programmable mechanical behavior.
