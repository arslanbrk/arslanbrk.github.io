About

Burak Arslan is a Doctoral Researcher in the Robotic Composites and Compositions Group at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems and the Multiscale Functional and Molecular Imaging Lab at ETH Zurich. He is a Doctoral Fellow of the Max Planck ETH Center for Learning Systems. He earned his BSc and MSc degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Bilkent University, Ankara. His current research explores magnetically actuated, untethered biomedical systems that harness jamming and programmable mechanical behavior.
